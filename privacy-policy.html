Privacy Policy — Solitaire Classic

Radian Pixel Apps · Last updated: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Android application Solitaire Classic ("the app"), published by Radian Pixel Apps ("we", "us"), handles information. We designed the app to collect as little as possible: it requires no account, no sign-in, and asks for no personal details.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information ourselves. The app has no registration, no login, and no form asking for your name, email address, or any other identifying detail.

Your game data — progress, statistics (games played, wins, streaks, best times), and settings such as sound mode and difficulty — is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or to any server, and it is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

2. Advertising

The app is free and supported by advertising. Ads are provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process certain information from your device, which can include:

  • Device information (such as model, operating system version, and language)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate (coarse) location derived from it
  • Ad interaction data (such as which ads were shown and whether they were tapped)

We do not receive or store this information; it is collected and processed by Google under its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from ads

3. Your advertising choices and consent

Where required by law — including in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland — the app shows a consent dialog (Google's User Messaging Platform) before ads are served. You can choose whether to allow personalised advertising; if you decline, ads may still be shown but will be non-personalised. Where the option is required, you can review or change your choice at any time from the app's Settings.

You can also reset or delete your device's advertising identifier at any time in your device settings under Privacy → Ads.

4. Children

The app is a classic card game suitable for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; as described above, the app collects no personal information from anyone.

5. Data security and retention

Because your game data never leaves your device, its security is governed by your device's own protections. We retain nothing on servers, because nothing is sent to us. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data.

6. Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles information — for example, by adding new features or services — we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected before they take effect.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or the app, contact us at: contact@radianpixelapps
